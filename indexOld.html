UMBC Game Developers Club

A professional development hub at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

Home Media Games Contact

The GDC was formed in the Fall 2005 semester by Eric Jordan with the goal of giving students from varied backgrounds a chance to work together and make games. Meetings are weekly: On Wednesday from 12:00-1:00 PM and on Friday at 1:00PM. The club room is located in Engineering 005A.
